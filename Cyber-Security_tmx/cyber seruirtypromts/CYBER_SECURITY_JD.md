# Job Description: Student Intern, Junior Cybersecurity Operations Analyst - TMX Group

## Role Overview
Are you an aspiring cybersecurity professional eager to launch your career in a dynamic and critical field? We are seeking a highly motivated Student Intern, Junior Cybersecurity Operations Analyst to join our Information Security Office. In this role, you will collaborate closely with our Security Operations Center (SOC) team, playing a vital part in protecting our digital assets against an ever-evolving threat landscape. This is an exceptional opportunity to gain hands-on experience, contribute to real-world security initiatives, and grow within a supportive environment.

- **Company:** TMX Group
- **Title:** Student Intern, Junior Cybersecurity Operations Analyst
- **Location:** Toronto, ON (Hybrid 2-3 days/week)
- **Reporting to:** Senior Manager, Security Operations Centre
- **Term:** May 4, 2026 - August 28, 2026 (4 months)
- **Salary:** $25 - $28/hour CAD

## Key Accountabilities
- **SOC Support & Administration:** Provide essential day-to-day administrative and operational support to the Security Operations Center (SOC) and Security Engineering teams.
- **Threat Monitoring & Analysis:** Assist in the proactive monitoring and analysis of security events and alerts from various security systems (e.g., SIEM, EDR, network logs) to detect and respond to potential threats.
- **Vulnerability & Threat Research:** Conduct research into emerging security technologies, cyber threats, vulnerabilities, and effective mitigation techniques.
- **Security Tool Management:** Support the maintenance, patching, and upgrade roadmap for critical security technologies and services.
- **Incident Support:** Contribute to initial triage and analysis during security incidents, helping to identify root causes and recommending containment strategies.
- **Automation & Scripting:** Develop and maintain scripts (e.g., Python, PowerShell) to automate routine security tasks, streamline workflows, and enhance operational efficiency.
- **Reporting & Metrics:** Assist in gathering data and generating metrics and Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for the SOC to provide actionable insights to senior management.
- **Documentation:** Periodically review and update information security procedures, architectural diagrams, standards, guidelines, and other technical documentation.

## Must Haves
- Currently enrolled in or recently completed 1st year of Bachelor's degree (Cybersecurity, CS, IT, etc.) or equivalent practical experience.
- Foundational understanding of info-sec principles (network, cloud - AWS/Azure/GCP, OS - Windows/Linux, IAM, data protection, attack vectors).
- Familiarity with security technologies: firewalls, IDS/IPS, SIEM platforms, EDR tools.
- Basic scripting skills (Python, PowerShell) for automation.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities.
- Excellent communication (written/verbal).
- Collaborative team player, self-motivated, and adaptable.

## Nice to Haves
- Cloud security experience (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
- Familiarity with NIST, ISO 27001.
- Cybersecurity extracurriculars (CTF, personal projects).
- Certifications: CompTIA Security+, CySA+, Google Cybersecurity.

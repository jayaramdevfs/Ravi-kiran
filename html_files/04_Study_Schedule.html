The 1-4-3 Rule of Prep

1 Hour
Company / JD Review
Research TMX, OSFI, financial cloud architectures. Memorize the Job Description.
4 Hours
Verbal Practice
DO NOT merely read. Speak out loud. Record yourself. Iterate on STAR stories.
3 Hours
Technical Gap-Filling
Crash courses on Kubernetes (AKS/EKS), Azure mapping, and networking ports.

DAY 1: THE FOUNDATION

Goal: Solidify your narrative, perfectly nail your intro, and deeply understand your projects.

09:00 - 10:00
Self-Intro Drill: Practice the 60s and 90s intro out loud until it sounds natural. Record and listen back.
10:00 - 11:30
Project Deep Dive: Stand at a whiteboard/paper and draw the architecture for SOC log analyzer and SOAR-Lite while explaining it.
11:30 - 12:30
TMX Alignment: Review TMX Group operations, OSFI regulations, and what high availability means for a stock exchange.
12:30 - 13:30
Lunch Break - Hydrate, clear your mind.
13:30 - 15:30
Tech Gap #1 (Kubernetes): Watch a 1-hour K8s crash course. Understand Pods, Deployments, Services, EKS vs AKS.
15:30 - 17:30
Tech Gap #2 (Azure): Review AWS-to-Azure service mappings. Focus on Entra ID (Azure AD), Resource Groups, and VNet basics.
Evening
Relax. Read exactly one article on "Cloud Migration in Financial Institutions."

DAY 2: THE SHARPENING

Goal: Rapid-fire recall, behavioral question mastery, and logistical prep.

09:00 - 11:00
Question Bank Drill (Sections 1-5): AWS, Azure, IaC, K8s, Networking. Read a question, answer out loud, check notes.
11:00 - 12:30
Cheat Sheet Memorization: Ports, protocol concepts, NIST 5-functions framework, DR (RTO/RPO). Write them down from memory.
12:30 - 13:30
Lunch Break - Walk outside. Get fresh air.
13:30 - 15:30
Behavioral Questions (STAR): Practice: Conflict, Mistake, Prioritization, Weakness, Why Hire You. Speak out loud!
15:30 - 17:00
Question Bank Drill (Sections 6-12): CI/CD, Scripting, Incident Response, TMX facts.
Evening
Interview Prep Routine: Lay out clothes, test Zoom/Teams camera & mic, put "Cheat Sheet" on split screen/print. Sleep early.

Study Tips & Focus Areas

  • You cannot study everything: Focus heavily on what's in your resume first (AWS, Terraform, Python). They will drill you on what you claim to know.
  • The "I don't know" technique: If asked something you don't know (e.g., Azure AKS), say: "I haven't used AKS in production, but based on my experience with Docker and AWS, I understand it manages the K8s control plane. My first step to deploy to it would be..."
  • Connect to Security: Always bring it back to your dual degree. TMX cares about security. Use the phrase: "With a security-first mindset..."

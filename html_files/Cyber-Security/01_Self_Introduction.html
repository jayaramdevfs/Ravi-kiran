1. THE 60-SECOND INTRO (Use if they say: "Give us a brief overview")

"Good morning/afternoon! I'm Ravi Kiran Kambhampati, a dedicated Cybersecurity professional with a strong foundation in SOC monitoring and threat detection based in Toronto. I've spent the last 2 years specializing across two Post-Graduate diplomas at York University: Cybersecurity Operations and Cloud Operations. I believe that in today's environment, a good security analyst must also understand the infrastructure they are protecting.

During my Cloud Engineer internship at TefoLogic, I managed IAM least-privilege policies, monitored AWS CloudTrail for unauthorized access attempts, and resolved production incidents. I've also built a comprehensive SOC Threat Detection Lab using the ELK stack, where I parsed Linux/Windows logs to identify brute-force attacks and abnormal user behavior.

I'm currently finalizing my prep for the CompTIA Security+ certification. What brings me to TMX is the critical nature of your financial systems. I'm eager to apply my SIEM knowledge and automation skills (Python/PowerShell) to help TMX maintain a secure and resilient exchange environment."

2. WHY TMX Group & WHY SOC?

  • The Core of the Economy: "TMX isn't just a business; it's the financial engine of Canada. Protecting it from threat actors is a high-stakes, mission-critical job that aligns with my background."
  • Hybrid Environment: "I know TMX is cloud-first but also maintains a hybrid footprint. My education in both CyberOps and CloudOps makes me uniquely comfortable monitoring across AWS and on-prem."
  • Continuous Learning: "TMX is known for its supportive culture. I'm looking for an environment where I can contribute to the SOC while learning from senior security engineers."

3. WHY SHOULD WE HIRE YOU?

  • The Builder-Analyst Bridge: "I don't just 'read' alerts; I understand the underlying system. My internship experience writing Terraform and monitoring CloudTrail means I can talk to the engineering teams effectively during triages."
  • Automation First Mindset: "I'm proficient in Python and PowerShell. I focus on automating routine security tasks to reduce noise and allow the team to focus on high-priority threats."
  • Dedication: "Completing two full Post-Graduate diplomas in specialized fields demonstrates my commitment to mastering the modern security landscape."

4. BIGGEST WEAKNESS

The Weakness: "Trying to handle everything alone."

The Script: "Early in my York University labs, I used to try to solve every complex log issue or networking bug myself to prove I could do it. I realized that in a real SOC, speed of containment is more important than solo troubleshooting. Now, I focus on the 20-minute rule: if I haven't cracked a complex triage issue in 20 minutes, I prepare my findings and escalate/collaborate with senior members. This ensures the organization stays protected and I learn faster."
